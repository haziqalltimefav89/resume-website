Summary of Experience

Through my four work experiences as an intern at Hospital Rehabilitasi Cheras, a Shopee warehouse worker, a waiter at Chicken Rice Shop, and a cashier/store keeper at Bila-Bila Mart I realized the importance of education and professional skills. During my internship, I became aware that with knowledge and dedication, one can secure a stable income without relying solely on physically demanding jobs like the ones I did during semester breaks to earn money. While I respect the value of hard work, I also understand that without education, continuing in labor intensive roles may eventually take a toll on one’s life. This experience strengthened my preference for office based work, where I can apply my skills, grow professionally, and build a sustainable career.
